Earlier today, I got hit by a virus attack on my laptop. This was infected by my pendrive which was caught on by another PC. Its a good thing I got a couple of anti-viruses running on my laptop; Avira AntiVir Personal and Panda.

I first ran Panda and it caught 23 viruses and all successfully quarantined. Then I ran Avira and so far it caught 1 virus and its still scanning. This is not how I wanna spend my weekend.
